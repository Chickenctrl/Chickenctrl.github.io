Synopsis

Suddenly, a mysterious monster "Giant" with a height of 15 meters appears in the world and preys on human beings.Faced with the crisis of survival, the remaining human beings built three huge protective walls to ensure the safety of the inner life circle.Human life has continued, enjoying a hundred years of peace in this isolated environment.Until Alan Yeager was ten years old, an unprecedented "super-giant" suddenly appeared.
After destroying the city gate with overwhelming force, it disappeared instantly, and giants rushed into the wall in groups to prey on humans.Alan saw his neighbor, partner, and mother being killed by giants one after another. With an indescribable hatred for giants, he vowed to kill all giants.The mother is dead, the father is missing, and his father may have mastered the mystery of the giant.Allen has a strong mental strength that no one else can match in the same period.Even if he has seen hell, he still challenges the giant and joins the long-awaited survey corps.

In the trailer of the January 2013 issue of the manga magazine "Bessatsu Shonen Magazine" (released on December 8, 2012), the official announcement was made that the TV animation of the manga "Attack on Titan" was confirmed, produced by WIT STUDIO. The first season began airing on April 6, 2013, with a total of 25 episodes. It was originally announced in the ending trailer of the movie "Attack on Titan Part 1 ~Red Lotus's Bow and Ya~" that the second season of the TV animation would be broadcast in 2016, but then it was announced that it was postponed, and it was finally scheduled to be broadcast in 2017. The second season began airing on April 1, 2017, with a total of 12 episodes. The third season started on July 22, 2018, and the second half (from 50 episodes) continued on April 28, 2019, with a total of 22 episodes. The final season was changed to be produced by MAPPA and started broadcasting on NHK General Channel at 0:10 (Japan time) on December 7, 2020. Animation Part.1 is the 60th to 75th words, all 16 words. Part.2 will start broadcasting on January 9, 2022, with episodes 76 to 87, and all 12 episodes. The end of the animation (Part.3) will be broadcast in 2023.
